Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Windspeed

How is Windspeed different from listing on Swiggy or Zomato?

Windspeed is your own branded food delivery platform. Unlike third-party services, you keep 100% of your profits, own customer data, control the user experience, and build your brand directly with customers.

What technical skills do I need to run Windspeed?

None! Windspeed is a fully managed solution. We handle all technical aspects including hosting, maintenance, and updates. You get a simple admin panel to manage your restaurant operations.

Can I customize the look and feel of my app?

Absolutely! Windspeed is a white-label solution, meaning you can fully brand it with your restaurant's logo, colors, and messaging. We offer various customization options to make it uniquely yours.

What payment methods are supported?

Windspeed supports Razorpay for online payments (credit/debit cards, UPI, netbanking, wallets) as well as cash on delivery (COD). You can also add custom payment methods based on your requirements.

How quickly can I get started with Windspeed?

We can have your custom-branded food delivery platform up and running in just 24-48 hours. The setup process is quick and we provide full onboarding support.
